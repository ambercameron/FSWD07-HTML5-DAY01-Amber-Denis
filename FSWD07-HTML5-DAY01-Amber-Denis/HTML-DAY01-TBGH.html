The Grand Budapest Hotel


The Grand Budapest Hotel is the 8th feature length film from critically acclaimed director Wes Anderson. It was the opening film for the 64th Berlin International Film Festival in 2014.

Plot Summary

The Grand Budapest Hotel recounts the adventures of Gustave H, a legendary concierge at a famous European hotel between the wars, and Zero Moustafa, the lobby boy who becomes his most trusted friend.
